Sitar player and composer Anoushka Shankar is one of the leading figures in World Music today. She is deeply rooted in the Indian classical music. She studied exclusively from the age of nine under her father and guru, the legendary Ravi Shankar. Among various accolades she has been thrice nominated for a Grammy Award and was the youngest, and first female, recipient of a House of Commons Shield from the British House of Parliament in 1999.
